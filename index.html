Getting
Out of the
Way

A practical framework for better church sound.

The book isn’t about sound systems.
It’s about learning how to listen.

Many church sound problems aren’t caused by bad equipment. They’re caused by solving the wrong problem.

It’s easy to get distracted by gear, presets, plugins, and endless advice online. But great audio rarely comes from more. It comes from knowing what matters, what doesn’t, and what to do about it.

The goal isn’t perfection.
The goal is getting out of the way.

Stay in touch.

No spam. Just meaningful updates from time to time.

About the author

Keegan McCarthy has spent the last fifteen years helping churches create clear, natural sound that serves worship—not distracts from it.

He started as a volunteer with no training and had to figure it out the hard way. This is the book he wishes someone had handed him when he started.
